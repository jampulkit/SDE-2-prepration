# Mock Interview Practice — Behavioral

## How to Use

Have someone ask you these questions (or record yourself answering). Time each answer to 2-3 minutes. Review against your story bank.

---

## Session 1: Amazon LP Focus (30 min)

| # | Question | Target LP | Time |
|---|---------|-----------|------|
| 1 | "Tell me about yourself." | — | 2 min |
| 2 | "Tell me about a time you took ownership of something outside your job description." | Ownership | 3 min |
| 3 | "Tell me about a time you disagreed with your manager. What did you do?" | Have Backbone | 3 min |
| 4 | "Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information." | Bias for Action | 3 min |
| 5 | "Tell me about a time you dove deep to find a root cause." | Dive Deep | 3 min |

**Common follow-ups to prepare for:**
- "What would you do differently?"
- "How did you measure the impact?"
- "What did you learn from this?"
- "How did the other person react?"

---

## Self-Evaluation Checklist

After each answer, check:
- [ ] Used STAR format (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- [ ] Kept answer under 3 minutes
- [ ] Used "I" not "we" for my specific contributions
- [ ] Included quantified results (%, time saved, revenue impact)
- [ ] Showed what I learned or how I grew
- [ ] Didn't badmouth anyone (manager, teammate, company)
- [ ] Had follow-up details ready when probed
