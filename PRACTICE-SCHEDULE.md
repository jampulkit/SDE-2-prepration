## Daily Routine
```
Morning:  DSA problems (fresh mind, hardest work first)
Afternoon: System design (read theory or practice a problem)
Evening:  Behavioral (practice one story out loud, 2-3 min timer)
Before bed: Review what you got wrong today (spaced repetition)
```

## Key Rules
1. **Never spend >45 min on one DSA problem.** Look at the solution, understand it, redo it tomorrow.
2. **Practice SD out loud.** Draw on paper/whiteboard. Talk through your design as if interviewing.
3. **Record yourself** doing behavioral answers. Listen back. Cut the fluff.
4. **Sleep 7+ hours.** Especially the night before the interview.
5. **Mock interviews > solo practice.** Find a partner or use Pramp/interviewing.io.
